Ning Miao (苗宁)

Department of Statistics, Oxford
Email: ning.miaostats.ox.ac.uk , miaoningpku.edu.cn

Ning Miao (left in the picture) is a Ph.D. student at the Department of Statistics, Oxford, supervised by Dr. Tom Rainforth and Prof. Yee Whye Teh. Before that, he was a researcher at Bytedance AI lab. He is currently working on natural language reasoning and generative models.
